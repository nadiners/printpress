Print/Press

1 In an effort to bridge the gap between designers and digital printers the Print/Press project will commission 12 independent designers to produce a 2019 calendar.

2 The calendar will address the theme of personalisation. In the era of big data, it is the contention of this project that work needs to be done to critically reflect on the implications of these techniques for the consumer.

3 The format of the publication is 6.8x8.5 inches and each contributer gets 1 double-page spread. The possible variable elements are:

  • TEXT
  • IMAGE/S
  • FONT/S
  • VECTOR/S
  • LAYOUT
  • COLOUR/S

4 Your contribution should include:

  • At least one consistent element
  • At least one variable element
  • As many versions of the variable element/s as you like (but no less than 12)

5 A list of the contributors and their work will be published on the Press/Print website.
contributions must be received by the 8th of June, 2018.
The calendar will be digitally printed using variable data techniques making it possible to produce a series of unique multiples for each design. (More to follow soon)
